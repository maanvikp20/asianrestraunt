Appetizers

Appetizers in Indian cuisine are light, flavorful dishes meant to stimulate the palate before the main course. They're typically served with chutneys or sauces.

  • Samosa

    A crispy, deep-fried pastry stuffed with spiced potatoes, peas, and sometimes meat. Often served with tamarind or mint chutney.

    Options

    1. Vegetarian
    2. Chicken
    3. Lamb

  • Paneer Tikka

    Cubes of paneer (Indian cottage cheese) marinated in yogurt and spices, grilled to perfection. Served with onion rings and lemon.
